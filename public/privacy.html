Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Executive Summary: We do not collect, store, or share your data. Everything stays on your device.

1. Data Collection

Memory Circle is designed as an "Offline-First" application. We do not operate a backend server, and we do not have a database of user information.

We do not collect:

  • Your personal details (name, email, age).
  • Your location data.
  • Your photos or audio recordings.
  • Your journal entries.

2. Data Storage

All data you create within the app (including text, images, and audio) is stored locally on your device using your browser's IndexedDB storage.

If you delete the app or clear your browser data/cache, your data will be permanently deleted unless you have created a backup.

3. Third-Party Services

We do not use third-party analytics services (like Google Analytics) or advertising networks.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@memorycircle.app
